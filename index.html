Our Vision

To be pioneers in innovation by supporting the Guests of Allah through the development of smart products that enhance their comfort and help them perform their rituals with ease and tranquility.

Our Mission

At "Rifad," we offer a practical and humane solution that elevates the pilgrimage experience by designing Ihrams equipped with a cooling material that reduces heat and ensures user comfort throughout their spiritual journey.

Our Goals

Pilgrim Comfort

Support the comfort of pilgrims during rituals by providing cooling-enhanced Ihrams.

Innovation

Innovate products that merge technology with spirituality, tailored to Hajj and Umrah conditions.

Sustainability

Promote sustainability by using safe and eco-friendly materials in manufacturing.

Target Audience

Pilgrims & Umrah Performers

Primary group in need of comfort during rituals in hot weather.

Hajj & Umrah Companies

Campaign operators that may collaborate to provide these products to their pilgrims.

Government & Organizations

Entities that may adopt the project for large-scale distribution.
